Caveats and Excluded Rows

Known limitations and rows excluded from top-line totals.

  • Coverage is directional. ManyVids Jan/Feb top-line earnings are manually backfilled from the monthly sales table, but title-level detail for those months is unavailable.
  • OnlyFans is sourced from Supercreator account exports and deduped by date so overlapping setup/backfill exports do not inflate totals. Native OF post and message statistics are supplemental content metrics and are excluded from top-line revenue totals.
  • Clips4Sale gross uses transaction totals; Clips4Sale net uses the validated 60% creator-share model. The May 2026 payout check landed within $4.02 of the model.
  • Curvage net uses daily purchases through the latest daily export, then item-level earnings for newer dates. Curvage gross is exact where item-level total_sales exists and estimated for older daily-only rows from the observed earnings / total_sales overlap. The manual title-count matrix feeds YTD clip/title intelligence by allocating canonical Curvage daily totals across titles; it is not added to platform totals.
  • ManyVids gross is estimated from official payout percentages by sale type; current captured rows are mostly Video at 60% and Club at 80%.
  • Cross-platform content winners use trusted video mappings synced from the reviewed master content workbook. Needs-review rows, photosets, and unmapped platform-specific titles stay out of the mapped rollup.
